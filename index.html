About
Us

We are Philippa and Jamie, AKA PB & J. We started this site to document our lives following a recent emigration from Brighton, UK, to Singapore.

In this blog, we'll be detailing our various travels around the continent, showcasing the photographs we take, and giving our opinions on the foodie delights that Singapore has to offer.

Philippa

  • Teacher and seasoned traveller. Just started teaching in an international school here.
  • Likes: Photography, sushi, live music, the colour purple.
  • Dislikes: Fresh coriander, airports.

Jamie

  • Relatively new recipient of the travel bug. Aspiring web developer
  • Likes: Cooking, skiing, writing, and telling terrible jokes.
  • Dislikes: Bananas, picking where to eat.
